Submissions

We are currently accepting submissions on a rolling basis. Deadlines for upcoming issues are:

  • July 1 2013 Fall
  • October 1 2013 Winter
  • January 1 2014 Spring

Guidelines:

  • Videos must be new, original works that have not been exhibited, screened or viewed online
  • Videos must be 2 or more channels
  • Channels can be arranged in any array
  • Each channel should be exactly the same duration (1-3 min ideal run time)

Viewers might watch your video in a bar, a restaurant, or while waiting in line. They have the control and responsibility of arranging the video image. Please note that the organic “messiness” of the viewing process is integral to the design of the magazine. Make it work for you.

To Submit:

There are two options for submitting video for consideration.

1) Encode each video channel separately and send us a diagram of how the channels should be arranged.

OR

2) Arrange your multi-channel piece into a single video file using video editing software. Denote where each screen begins/ends.

Video files that are 25mb or under can be emailed directly. Larger files should be password protected and uploaded to Vimeo.

Send your submission/links to: submissions.tabularasa@gmail.com.
