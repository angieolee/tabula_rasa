What is Tabula Rasa?

Tabula Rasa is a video magazine based in San Francisco that puts art into the hands of the viewer. Each issue features multi-screen videos that can be watched by linking phones, tablets, or any portable video device. Tabula Rasa seeks to promote avant-garde video and film by artists both national and international, as well as create a new venue for experiencing multi-channel video work.
